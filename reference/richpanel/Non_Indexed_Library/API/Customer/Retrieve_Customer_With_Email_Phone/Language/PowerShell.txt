Retrieve a Customer with Email/Phone
get
https://api.richpanel.com/v1/customers/{type}/{id}

Language: PowerShell

$headers=@{}
$headers.Add("accept", "application/json")
$headers.Add("x-richpanel-key", "test")
$response = Invoke-WebRequest -Uri 'https://api.richpanel.com/v1/customers/type/id' -Method GET -Headers $headers
